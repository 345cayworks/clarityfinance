# Terms of Use Draft

This draft is for product planning and legal review. It is not legal advice.

## Purpose

Clarity Finance provides educational and planning tools that help users organize financial information, understand readiness indicators, prepare lender conversations, create reports, and request advisor review.

## No Financial or Investment Advice

Clarity Finance does not provide financial advice, investment advice, tax advice, legal advice, or accounting advice. Investment and dividend tools are for education and planning only and do not recommend buying, selling, or holding any security.

## Not a Loan Approval

Clarity Finance does not approve loans, make credit decisions, provide underwriting decisions, or guarantee lender outcomes. Readiness scores, ranges, reports, and summaries are planning estimates based on user-entered information.

## User-Entered Data Responsibility

Users are responsible for entering accurate, complete, and current information. Outputs may be incomplete, inaccurate, or misleading if user-entered data is incomplete or incorrect.

## Final Lender Underwriting Required

Any loan, mortgage, refinance, credit product, or bank decision is subject to lender review, verification, underwriting, documentation, credit criteria, property valuation, and applicable law outside Clarity Finance.

## No Guarantee of Outcomes

Clarity Finance does not guarantee approval, qualification, returns, savings, debt reduction, investment performance, advisor outcomes, bank decisions, or any financial result.

## Premium Tools

Premium tools may include advanced planning calculators, investment what-if analysis, dividend reinvestment projections, loan readiness modules, and other features. Premium access does not change the educational nature of the tools and does not create professional advice or guaranteed outcomes.

## Advisor Review Boundaries

Advisor requests allow users to share selected information for review. Advisor review does not create a loan approval, investment recommendation, or underwriting decision. Advisors may require additional information and may provide general guidance subject to their own licensing, agreements, and compliance obligations.

## Bank Sharing Consent

Clarity Finance requires explicit consent before sharing selected readiness summaries, financial snapshots, reports, or supporting information with an advisor, bank, or financial institution. Users should review the recipient and shared scope before consenting.
